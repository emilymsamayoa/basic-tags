Giraffes: The Beautiful Giant

What about them?

Giraffes are the tallest land mammal, ranging in heights from 14-19 feet. They are herbivores who dwell primarily in East Africa across 15 countries.


There are nine subspecies of giraffes:

  • Nubian
  • West African
  • Kordofan
  • South African
  • Angolan
  • Masai
  • Thornicroft's
  • Rothchild's
